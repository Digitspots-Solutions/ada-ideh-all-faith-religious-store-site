About Us

Ada-Ideh All Faith Religious Store is a leading manufacturer and supplier of religious items in Idemili North, Anambra State. We understand the sacred importance of worship items in the spiritual lives of Nigerians.

From our facility on Azodo Street, Npor Agu, we serve communities throughout Nigeria with products crafted with reverence and attention to detail — for Christian, Islamic, and indigenous African worship traditions alike.

Our Products

Christian Items

Crosses, rosaries, communion sets, baptismal items, church candles, holy water fonts, and devotional materials.

Islamic Items

Prayer mats, tasbihs, Quranic stands, Islamic wall art, attar oils, miswak sticks, and Ramadan essentials.

Traditional & Interfaith

Spiritual candles, incense, prayer beads, meditation items, and ceremonial accessories for all traditions.

Wholesale & Custom Orders

Bulk manufacturing for churches, mosques, and retailers. Custom designs available. Nigeria-wide delivery.
